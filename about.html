Kelvin Mutua is a rising filmmaker, photographer, and designer from Kenya, passionate about telling authentic stories that resonate with local and global audiences. Starting from humble beginnings, Kelvin’s journey in the world of film and photography has been fueled by a deep love for visual storytelling.

Inspired by the vibrant culture and rich traditions of Kenya, Kelvin’s work explores themes of identity, community, and social change. His passion for capturing powerful narratives is reflected in his diverse portfolio, ranging from short films to commercial and documentary projects.

With an eye for detail and a unique perspective, Kelvin combines technical skills with a creative vision that brings out the raw emotions and experiences of his subjects. As a young artist in a fast-evolving industry, his dedication to his craft continues to drive him to innovate and push boundaries, making a mark on both the Kenyan and international film scene.
